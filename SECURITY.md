# Security Policy

## Reporting a Vulnerability

Please report suspected vulnerabilities privately to the repository owner or the I-Magnus engineering contact. Do not open public issues for secrets, authentication bypasses, payment flaws, or student data exposure.

Include:

- A concise description of the issue
- Affected route, file, or workflow
- Reproduction steps when safe to share
- Any evidence redacted of student data, tokens, or credentials

We will prioritize reports that may affect authentication, payments, student data, or administrative access.
